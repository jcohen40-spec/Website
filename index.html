Statistics

I enjoy the methodical process of statistical thought, such as formulating studies and expermental designs. Recently, I have been enjoying learning more about causal inference and how to isolate causal effects by understanding confounders, backdoor paths, and modern causal methods. One field that has caught my eye is statistical consulting, and I am interested in learning more. That being said, I am still open to all kinds of statistical work.

While I enjoy and am capable of working with other people, I am also a strong, independent worker. My hope is to engage in some sort of client-facing work at some point in my career, as I believe it would be fulfilling for my work to be directly actionable towards other people. I hope data science and statistics can continue to aid in the process of developing human knowledge, growth, and understanding.

Data Analyticss

To me data analytics is the modern successor of statistics. With the advent so much more information at our fingertips, it is necessary to understand how to organize, manage, and analyze data. Personally, the more enjoyable applications data science are in the statistical vein. Data science is a necessary tool in order to engage with statistics today, and I am proficient in some tools such as R and can also use python. I hope to keep growing my skills in this department.
